Reflective Self Portrait





Created using charcoal, this 33”x40” self-portrait was an exploration in materiality in a physical and metaphorical sense. The subject is covered in pearls and jewels yet, holds a humdrum expression contrasting the lavishness associated with the items in her possession. I enjoyed the challenge of attempting to create on a large scale in a new medium. The piece forced me to home in on technical elements of creating and expanded my understanding of drawing basics.
