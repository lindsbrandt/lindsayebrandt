Jeanz: Waste in the Fashion Industry

This installation was created as part of the Systems on the Brink exhibition held at Studio 2-3. It was created in collaboration with to other designers Amanda Houttekier and Julia Spewak. Together we explored the fast fashion system and its effects on our global environment. We created buzz phrases like “Buy it now, throw it away later” and “pile them high, sell them cheap” to mimiced flash sale signs which we then printed onto up cycled pieces of denim. We also created a full window display that expressed our message out to be viewed on the street, similar to a clothing stores window sales.
