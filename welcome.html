All Are Welcome

This poster was commissioned by a local Virginia educator for his classroom. He wanted to have a poster that helps foster inclusivity and reinforces a safe environment. I created this 17"x11" poster using a risograph to obtain interesting texture and color layering to compliment the letter forms. I wanted to keep the overall piece fun to fit the vibe of a high school classroom but made sure not to take away from the importance of the message.
