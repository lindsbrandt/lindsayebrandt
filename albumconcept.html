Positions - Album Concept Work

The goal was to create a new concept design for the singer's sixth album. The original design felt unfinished and under developed for an album created later on in an artists career. It was important to keep the initial themes and color palette, while expanding and producing a more fleshed out concept that reflects the care-free and self exploration of the albums music. The design includes the vinyls front and back covers, the inside of the opened sleeve, the vinyl protector sleeves, and the vinyls themselves.
